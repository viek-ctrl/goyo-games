Our Story

We build games for everyone, everywhere

GOYO Games started with a small crew of mobile players who kept asking the same question: why do so many games make you work before you get to have fun? So we set out to make the opposite — games you can pick up between a bus stop and your front door, understand in five seconds, and grin about for the rest of the day.

Today we're still that same small, scrappy team — just with a lot more puzzles solved, candies matched, and bubbles popped along the way.

Our mission

Make joyful, accessible games that fit into anyone's pocket-sized moments.

Fun first

Every decision starts with one question: is this actually fun?

Built for mobile

Designed from day one for touchscreens, short sessions, and big smiles.

Player obsessed

We watch, listen, and iterate based on what real players love.
